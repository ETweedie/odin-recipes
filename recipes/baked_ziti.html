Baked Ziti

Description

The following recipe for a delicious baked ziti recipe. This will be a crowd favorite whenever you make this, it is also always a hit with the family. The recipe can be made with or without meat. Anyone who tries this dish will enjoy it and will most definitely ask for the recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dry ziti pasta
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 (26 ounce) jars spaghetti sauce
  • 6 ounces provolone cheese, sliced
  • 1 1/2 cups sour cream
  • 6 ounces mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
