Baked Ziti

Description

The following recipe for a delicious baked ziti recipe. This will be a crowd favorite whenever you make this, it is also always a hit with the family. The recipe can be made with or without meat. Anyone who tries this dish will enjoy it and will most definitely ask for the recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dry ziti pasta
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 (26 ounce) jars spaghetti sauce
  • 6 ounces provolone cheese, sliced
  • 1 1/2 cups sour cream
  • 6 ounces mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add ziti pasta, and cook until al dente, about 8 min
  3. Brown ground beef and onion in large skillet over medium heat, stir spaghetti sauce and simmer 15 min, preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C), butter a 9x13 inch baking dish
  4. Spread 1/2 of ziti in the bottom of the prepared dish
  5. Top with Provolone cheese
  6. Top with sour cream
  7. Add 1/2 of the meat sauce, remaining ziti, mozzarella cheese, and remaining meat sauce. Top with Parmesan cheese
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until heated through and cheese have melted, about 30 min
